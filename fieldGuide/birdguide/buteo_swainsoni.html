Origin: Native Status: Not Applicable Occurrence: Occasional
Description: Length 53 cm. In flight, this bird is distinguished from other buteos by its long, narrow, pointed wings. Adult plumage is highly variable. Generally, adults have an obvious dark bib, light underparts, and pale uppertail coverts. Bill is small. Call is a shrill, aggravated-sounding "kearrrrrrr". Found in agricultural lands, riparian forests and grasslands. Diet consists of small mammals, insects and birds.
