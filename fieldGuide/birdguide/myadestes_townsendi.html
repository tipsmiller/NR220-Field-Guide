Description: Length 22 cm. This bird is most easily identified by its long, slender body. Plumage is slate grey overall with a bright white eye ring. Outertail coverts are white, obvious in flight. Call is a high "eek" note. Song is melodious and complex with no distinct pattern; heard year-round. Found primarily in coniferous forests. Diet consists of insects and berries.
