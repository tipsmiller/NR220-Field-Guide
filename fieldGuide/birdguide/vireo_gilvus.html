Sounds: No Sounds Available
Image: perched
Community: Mountain Shrub, Ponderosa Pine, Lodgepole pine,
Origin: Native Status: Not Applicable Occurrence: Rare
Description: Length 14 cm. Olive-grey above and white underneath, with pale yellow flanks. Warbling Vireos have a slight dusky post-ocular stripe, and a prominent, white eyebrow extending just beyond the eye. Song is delivered in long, melodious, warbling phrases. Most common in apsen and riparian forests. Diet is insectivorous.
