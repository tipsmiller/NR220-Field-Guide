Sounds: mating, typical,

Origin: Native Status: Not Applicable Occurrence: Occasional
Description: Length 56 cm. This bird is our most common buteo. Plumage is extremely variable. Generally, adults show a dark streaked belly band on light brown underparts. Back is dark brown, slightly lighter on the head. Tail is dark reddish above. Call is distinctive; a harsh, descending "keeeer". Habitat is variable. Found in woodlands, prairies, agricultural areas, and desert. Diet consists of rodents and small mammals.
