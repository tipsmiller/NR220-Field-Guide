Description: Length 25 cm. The male Brewer's Blackbird is identified by yellow eyes, a purplish gloss on his head and neck, greenish gloss on the wings and black overall. The female?s eyes and body are grey-brown with darker wings. Call is a harsh "check". Song is an airy "que-ee" or "k-see". Common in shrublands, riparian woodlands and open habitats. Diet consists of insects, crustaceans and seeds.
