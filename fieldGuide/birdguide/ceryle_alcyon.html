Description: Length 33 cm. This stocky, large headed bird is the only kingfisher in most of North America. The Belted Kingfisher is easily identified by its ragged head crest and large bill. Both sexes have a slate blue back, head and breast brand. Belly and undertail coverts are white, and females have rusty belly bands and flanks. Call is a loud, dry rattle. Common along streams, ponds, estuaries, and other bodies of water. Diet consist mainly of fish.
