Community: Mountain Shrub, Ponderosa Pine, Riparian,
Origin: Native Status: Not Applicable Occurrence: Rare
Description: Length 13cm. This small warbler has an olive green back, and a light olive to yellow underside. Females have a gray-brown hood, and males have a darker gray hood. This warbler has a distinctive white crescent above and below the eye. Call is a sharp, harsh "tsik", and song is a buzzy trill ending in a downslur.
