Origin: Native Status: Not Applicable Occurrence: Rare
Description: Length 56-64 cm. This bird only nests near water. Plumage is dark brown above, white below with light streaking on the breast. Head is also white with a prominent dark eye stripe. In flight, dark carpel patches are obvious and the undersides of the wings are brown with heavy white barring. Call is a series of loud, whistled "kyews". Found near large bodies of water. Diet consists almost exclusively of fish.
