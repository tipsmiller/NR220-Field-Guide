Sounds: typical,

Origin: Native Status: Not Applicable Occurrence: Occasional
Description: Length 13 cm. Like other members of the vireo family, this bird is characterized by a sturdy bill that is slightly curved down at the tip. Head and nape are slate grey with an eye ring that extends through the forehead. Tail feather edges are white with white wingbars. Grey on the sides of the breast. Song is varied with deliberate pauses, often repeating. Diet is primarily insectivorous.
